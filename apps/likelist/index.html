Privacy Policy for LikeList

Effective date: April 21, 2026

LikeList helps users save places, dishes, notes, ratings, and photos. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may process and how it is used.

Information We Process

The app may process the following types of information:

  • account information when you sign in with Google, Apple, or another supported sign-in method;
  • content created by you, including saved places, dishes, categories, notes, ratings, and photos;
  • technical app data required for synchronization, data recovery, analytics, and crash diagnostics;
  • purchase and subscription information if you subscribe to Premium through Google Play.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • provide the core features of the app;
  • synchronize your data across your devices;
  • restore your data after signing in again;
  • process and verify Premium subscriptions;
  • improve app stability and fix errors.

Local Storage and Cloud Sync

Your data may be stored locally on your device. If you sign in and use sync features, your data may also be transmitted to and stored in cloud services used by the app, including Firebase.

Photos

You may add photos to your saved items. These photos are used to display your content inside the app and, if sync is enabled, to synchronize your content across your devices.

Payments and Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Google Play. The app may receive technical purchase information, such as the product ID and purchase token, to verify Premium access. Payment details such as your card number are not shared with the app.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by third-party services required for the app to function, including Google Firebase, Google Play Services, and Google Play Billing.

Children

LikeList is not specifically intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Deletion

You can delete content you created inside the app. To request deletion of your account or data associated with it, please contact us by email.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
