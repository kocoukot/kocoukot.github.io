LikeList Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your LikeList account directly from the app settings. If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion of your account and associated data by email at kocoukot@gmail.com.

If you contact us by email, please include the email address or sign-in method associated with your LikeList account.

Data that will be deleted

  • your account-related app data;
  • saved places, dishes, notes, ratings, categories, addresses, and coordinates that you stored in the app;
  • photos uploaded or synchronized by the app;
  • cloud-synchronized app data associated with your account;
  • friends-related profile, notifications, and request data associated with your account, except where limited retention is required for security or abuse prevention.

Data that may be retained

Some technical, billing, analytics, crash, or security records may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or accounting purposes.

Retention period

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Data retained for legal, security, billing, or accounting purposes may be kept for as long as required by applicable law or platform requirements.

Partial data deletion

You can delete saved places, dishes, notes, ratings, and photos directly inside the LikeList app without deleting your account.
