LikeList Support

Support page for App Store and Google Play

If you need help with LikeList, contact us by email: kocoukot@gmail.com

What We Can Help With

  • sign-in and account issues;
  • sync and backup problems;
  • maps, address search, and location permission issues;
  • friend requests, notifications, and social visibility issues;
  • Premium subscription and restore issues;
  • data deletion requests;
  • bug reports and general feedback.

When Contacting Support

Please include your device model, platform version, app version, and a short description of the issue. If the problem is related to a purchase, include the purchase platform and the approximate purchase date.

For account deletion, first use the in-app delete account flow in LikeList settings. If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request by email instead.
