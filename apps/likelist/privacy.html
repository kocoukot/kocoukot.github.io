Privacy Policy for LikeList

Effective date: May 13, 2026

LikeList helps users save places, dishes, notes, ratings, and photos. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may process and how it is used.

Information We Process

The app may process the following types of information:

  • account information when you sign in with Google or Apple;
  • content created by you, including saved places, dishes, notes, ratings, categories, and photos;
  • social data used to support friends features, such as pair codes, friend requests, profile visibility, friend lists, notifications, and friend-facing read-only snapshots of places and dishes that you choose to share;
  • technical app data required for synchronization, notifications, data recovery, analytics, and crash diagnostics;
  • purchase and subscription information if you subscribe to Premium through Google Play or the Apple App Store.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • provide the core features of the app;
  • synchronize your data across your devices;
  • restore your data after signing in again;
  • support friends features, including friend requests, friend notifications, and showing selected places or dishes to friends in read-only form when you choose to share them;
  • process and verify Premium subscriptions;
  • improve app stability and fix errors.

Local Storage and Cloud Sync

Your data may be stored locally on your device. If you sign in and use sync features, your data may also be transmitted to and stored in cloud services used by the app, including Firebase.

Location Data and Maps

If you use address search, map selection, or the "my location" action, LikeList may request access to your device location. Location access is used to help center the map, improve address lookup, and let you save places with coordinates when you choose to do so.

Location access is not required for the basic use of the app. The app is not intended to use background location tracking. You may also enter an address manually without granting location permission.

If you save a place with an address or coordinates, that address and those coordinates become part of your content and may be synchronized across your devices when sync is enabled.

Friends and Social Features

If you use friends features, LikeList may process your social profile, pair code, friend requests, notifications, and read-only snapshots of places and dishes that you choose to make visible to friends. Private places that you do not mark as visible to friends are not intended to appear in friend snapshots.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app may process push notification tokens and notification payloads needed to deliver social events such as friend requests or accepted requests.

Photos

You may add photos to your saved items. These photos are used to display your content inside the app and, if sync is enabled, to synchronize your content across your devices.

Payments and Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by platform billing systems such as Google Play and the Apple App Store. The app may receive technical purchase information, such as the product ID, purchase token, or transaction ID, to verify Premium access. Payment details such as your card number are not shared with the app.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by third-party services required for the app to function, including Google Firebase, Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google Play Services, Google Maps, Google Places, Google Play Billing, Sign in with Apple, and App Store billing services.

Children

LikeList is not specifically intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Deletion

You can delete content you created inside the app. You can also delete your account from the app settings. If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion of your account or associated data by email.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
